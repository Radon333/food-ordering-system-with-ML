About Us

We are a group of 3 students and we created this Web Apllication as our Sem-3 project.

The idea behind the automated canteen system is to simply the ordering process for both the customer and the canteen staff.

It provides an e-menu through which the customers can order without waiting in long queues. In addition to that we are providng recommendation thorugh our content filtering algorithm for the best user experience with a visual confirmation that the order has been placed.
